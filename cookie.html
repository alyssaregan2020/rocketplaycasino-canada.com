2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, to provide a personalized experience, and to supply reporting information to the website owners.

Cookies can be:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary and expire once you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.
  • First-party Cookies: Set by the website you are visiting (us).
  • Third-party Cookies: Set by a domain other than the one you are visiting (e.g., our analytics or advertising partners).
