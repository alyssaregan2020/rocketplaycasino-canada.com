3. Recognizing a Potential Problem

Responsible gambling is about awareness. If you are concerned about your gambling habits, please consider the following questions honestly:

  • Do you spend more money or time gambling than you originally intended?
  • Have you ever hidden the extent of your gambling from people you care about?
  • Are you trying to win back money you have lost by gambling more?
  • Has gambling caused problems in your personal relationships, work, or studies?
  • Do you gamble to escape from stress, boredom, or other problems?
  • Have you ever borrowed money or sold anything to get money to gamble?

If you answered "yes" to one or more of these questions, it may indicate a gambling problem. We urge you to speak to someone and to use our self-exclusion tool immediately.
